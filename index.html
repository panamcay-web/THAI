What Is the Thailand Digital Arrival Card?

The TDAC (Thailand Digital Arrival Card) is a mandatory entry requirement for all travelers entering Thailand. It must be completed before departure and presented at immigration. By using our service, we ensure your application is submitted correctly and on time, allowing you to enjoy your holiday without worrying about paperwork complications.
