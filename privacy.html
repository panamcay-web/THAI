2. What data we collect

We collect only the information required to deliver the TDAC processing service:

  • Technical data: IP address, browser user-agent, and referrer URL.
  • Marketing attribution data: (UTM tags) when present in the URL.

3. Why we use your data

  • To process and submit your TDAC application to the Thai immigration authorities on your behalf.
  • To send you the TDAC document, payment confirmation, and necessary service updates.
  • To prevent fraud and verify payment integrity.
  • To improve our service through aggregated analytics on usage patterns.
  • To comply with tax, accounting, and other legal obligations.

4. Who we share data with

  • Thai Immigration authorities: To issue your official arrival document.
  • Our payment provider: For secure processing of your transaction.
  • Our email delivery provider: To send confirmation messages and your completed TDAC.
  • Authorities and courts: Only if legally required.

We do not sell personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data retention

We keep order data only for as long as required by applicable tax, accounting, and immigration record-keeping laws. After this period, data is securely deleted or anonymised.
