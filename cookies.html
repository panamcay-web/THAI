Cookies Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help websites remember information about your visit — like your language preferences, session data, or analytics identifiers to ensure a smooth user experience.

Cookies we use

Strictly necessary

These cookies are essential for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled:

  • Session cookie: Keeps your session active while you navigate our site.
  • Checkout session: Stores your TDAC application data temporarily so you can move between steps without losing information.
  • CSRF token: Protects our forms and your data from cross-site request forgery attacks.

Analytics

We use minimal analytics to understand how the website performs and to ensure our service is reliable. These cookies are set only with your consent:

  • Aggregated usage data: Tracks page views, device types, and the country of origin of our visitors.
  • Traffic source: Captures UTM parameters from your arrival URL to help us understand which channels our users come from.

Note: We do not perform cross-site tracking or build individual user profiles.

Marketing

If you arrive at our website through an advertising link with UTM parameters, we store those parameters in session storage (not as a cookie) to attribute your order to the correct campaign. This process does not identify you personally.

Managing cookies

You can disable or delete cookies in your browser settings at any time. Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies will prevent the TDAC application form from working correctly.

In most browsers, you can manage settings here:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data.
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data.
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy.

Updates

We update this policy when the technology or cookies we use change. The date at the top reflects the latest revision.
